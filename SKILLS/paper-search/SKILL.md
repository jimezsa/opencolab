description: Search, read, compare, and summarize AI research papers with clear evidence links. Use when agent tasks require literature discovery and synthesis.
---

Use this skill to structure paper-focused tasks.

Steps:
1. Find relevant papers and links.
2. Extract claim, method, and results.
3. Compare agreement/disagreement across papers.
4. Produce concise summary with citations and open questions.
